# 📌 Business Insights

Based on SQL analysis and exploratory data analysis (EDA), the following business insights were identified:

- Active customers significantly outnumber churned customers.
- Basic subscription plan shows the highest churn rate.
- Customers with lower Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV) are more likely to churn.
- Low CSAT scores indicate a higher probability of customer churn.
- Monthly charges have a moderate relationship with customer lifetime value.
- Premium and Standard plans have better customer retention than the Basic plan.
- Female customers slightly outnumber male customers in the dataset.
- Longer customer tenure generally corresponds to higher CLTV.

---

# 💡 Recommendations

To reduce customer churn and improve customer satisfaction:

1. Improve customer support quality for low CSAT customers.
2. Introduce loyalty rewards for long-term customers.
3. Review pricing strategy for Basic subscription plans.
4. Target high-risk customers with personalized retention campaigns.
5. Monitor customer satisfaction regularly.
6. Improve onboarding experience for new customers.
7. Increase engagement through personalized offers.
8. Track churn KPIs using interactive dashboards.
